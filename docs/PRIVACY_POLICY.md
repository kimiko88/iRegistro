- Absences (assenze)

- Email address

### 2.3 Teacher Data
- Full name
- Email address
- Employee ID
- Subjects taught
- Classes assigned

### 2.4 Usage Data
- Login timestamps
- IP addresses (for security)
- Device information
- Access logs

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

- **Legal Obligation** (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR): School administrative requirements under Italian law
- **Public Interest** (Art. 6(1)(e) GDPR): Educational mission
- **Consent** (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): Optional services (photos, communications)
- **Legitimate Interest** (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): Security and fraud prevention

## 4. How We Use Your Data

- **Academic Management**: Recording marks, absences, and academic progress
- **Communication**: School-to-parent messaging, notifications
- **Document Generation**: Report cards, certificates, attestations
- **Security**: Access control, audit logging, fraud prevention
- **Legal Compliance**: Meeting Italian school law requirements

## 5. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell your data. We share data only with:

- **School Staff**: Teachers, administrators (on a need-to-know basis)
- **Italian Authorities**: When required by law (e.g., Ministry of Education)
- **Service Providers**: Cloud hosting (AWS/Azure), under strict contracts
- **Parents/Guardians**: Student academic information

**No third-party marketing or advertising.**

## 6. Data Security

### 6.1 Encryption
- **In Transit**: TLS 1.3 (HTTPS everywhere)
- **At Rest**: AES-256-GCM for sensitive fields (tax code, phone, address)
- **Passwords**: Bcrypt hashing

### 6.2 Access Control
- Role-based permissions
- Two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Audit logging of all data access

### 6.3 Infrastructure
- SOC 2 Type II certified cloud providers
- Daily backups
- Disaster recovery plan

## 7. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period | Legal Basis |
|-----------|------------------|-------------|
| Student Marks | 5 years | Italian School Law (DPR 122/2009) |
| Absences | 5 years | Italian School Law |
| Messages | 2 years | School Policy |
| Audit Logs | 1 year | GDPR Art. 32 |
| Temporary Files | 7 days | Data Minimization |

After retention periods, data is automatically deleted.

## 8. Your Rights (GDPR Art. 12-22)

### 8.1 Right to Access (Art. 15)
Request a copy of your data in JSON, CSV, or XML format.

**How**: Email dpo@registro.it or use in-app export feature.

### 8.2 Right to Rectification (Art. 16)
Correct inaccurate personal information.

**How**: Contact your school administrator.

### 8.3 Right to Erasure (Art. 17)
Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).

**How**: Submit deletion request via app or email dpo@registro.it.  
**Note**: Academic records must be retained for 5 years by law.

### 8.4 Right to Data Portability (Art. 20)
Receive your data in a machine-readable format.

**How**: Use in-app export or email dpo@registro.it.

### 8.5 Right to Object (Art. 21)
Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

**How**: Email dpo@registro.it.

### 8.6 Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7)
For optional features (photos, communications).

**How**: Manage consents in app settings.

## 9. Consent Management
